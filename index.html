FAQ

Questions counselors ask us

Is student data safe? What about FERPA?

Student data stays inside Menteco and is used only to power each student's own guidance. Readiness scores and alerts are computed in-system with transparent, deterministic math. We don't run ads, and we never sell data. Compliance documentation (FERPA, COPPA, Student Privacy Pledge) is available for school procurement reviews.

Does the AI make decisions about my students?

No. All scores and alerts come from auditable rules and math. AI is used for one thing: turning those facts into well-written briefings and message drafts — which you review and send yourself. If AI is unavailable, everything still works with plain templates.

Do I have to replace Naviance or my school's system?

No. Menteco works alongside existing systems. Bring your caseload in via CSV import or quick manual entry — most counselors are fully set up within an afternoon.

What do students see?

Students get their own app: a readiness score measured against their own plan, a timeline of what's coming, their goals and applications, and chat with you. They're never ranked against other students.

Can I try it with a few students first?

Yes — the Free plan covers 3 students forever, no card required. Most counselors pilot with a handful of seniors, then upgrade when application season ramps up.
